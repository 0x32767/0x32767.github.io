What's my opinion of ChatGPT and do I use it?

The short answer is no. I am not a big fan of ChatGPT and it's influence on programming. I still write all my code by hand with help from stack overflow. The reason being is that I prefer to know exactley what the code is doing and why it is doing it, ChatGPT feels like an unncessery layer of abstraction that separates you from the code.
